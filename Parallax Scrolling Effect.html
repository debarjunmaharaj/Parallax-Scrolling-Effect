The content here scrolls normally. As you continue to scroll, you will notice that the background image in the previous section remains fixed in place. This contrast between the scrolling content and the fixed background is what creates the parallax effect. It adds a sense of depth and can make a webpage feel more dynamic and engaging.

This technique is very popular in modern web design, especially for landing pages and storytelling websites. It's achieved with just a few lines of CSS, making it a powerful yet simple tool for developers. The key is the `background-attachment: fixed;` property.
